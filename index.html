Therapy That Works

I believe effective therapy combines proven techniques with a genuine therapeutic relationship. My approach is integrative, drawing from multiple evidence-based modalities to create a treatment plan that fits your unique needs.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identify and shift unhelpful thought patterns that impact your emotions and behaviors.

Trauma-Focused Approaches

Process difficult experiences safely with specialized trauma-informed techniques.

Mindfulness-Based Strategies

Develop present-moment awareness and emotional regulation skills.

Relational & Psychodynamic Work

Frequently Asked Questions

Who do you work with?

I work with adolescents (ages 14+) and adults facing a variety of challenges including anxiety, trauma, depression, relationship issues, and life transitions.

Do you accept insurance?

Please contact me directly to discuss insurance options and out-of-network benefits. I'm happy to provide documentation for reimbursement.

How long is each session?

Standard sessions are 50 minutes. We'll meet weekly initially, with the option to adjust frequency as we progress.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, everything discussed in therapy is confidential with limited exceptions (risk of harm to self or others, child/elder abuse, court order).

How do I know if we're a good fit?

The therapeutic relationship is crucial to success. I offer a free consultation call so we can discuss your needs and see if we'd work well together.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes, I offer secure telehealth sessions for clients who prefer meeting virtually or need that flexibility.

Ready to Get Started?

Taking the first step toward therapy can feel daunting. I offer a free 15-minute consultation call to answer your questions and see if we're a good fit.
