Meet Zainub

Creating Spaces for Connection & Growth

Hi, I'm Dr. Zainub Mallick. From a young age, I've been fascinated by the brain, the mind, and the beautiful complexity of human connection.

My journey started at the University of Houston (Go Coogs!), took me to New York for graduate school, California for my internship, and brought me back home to Houston for my post-doctoral fellowship. I'm overjoyed to give back to the community that raised me.

What drives me? Creating a space where you can safely explore all the parts of who you are—without judgment, with full acceptance of your experiences. I believe therapy is where healing begins by exploring what you're carrying: both the burdens and the strengths.

My Core Belief

I take a relational, strengths-based approach, always looking for what you're doing right and building upon those successes. By combining our strengths, we create a supportive environment where real change can occur.

My Approach

How We'll Work Together

Therapy is a space where healing begins—not through judgment, but through collaboration, strength-building, and genuine connection.

01

Team Approach

We're partners in this journey. Rather than judgment or discipline, I see us as a team tackling challenges together. Your input shapes everything we do.

02

Strengths-Based

I'm always looking for what you're doing right and building upon those successes. We combine our strengths to create real, lasting change.

03

Tailored to You

Whether through traditional talk therapy, play-based interventions, or behavioral techniques, I adjust my methods to best serve you and your family's unique needs.

04

Full Acceptance

This is a space to safely explore the many parts of who you are—without judgment, with full acceptance of your experiences and identity.
